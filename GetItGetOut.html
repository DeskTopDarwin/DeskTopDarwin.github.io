Get It & Get Out

A Unity VR game based around grappling hook to move around. The goal of the game is to bring back as many treasures as possible to the extraction point before the lava reaches the top of the volcano. The map will get harder and harder to move in as the lava reaches the top and as building collapses.

My Role

In this Team of 5 using Agile Scrum work methodology with Jira, my role consisted of making the destructible buildings, the level of detail (LOD), and the building of the map. The destruction of buildings was a challenge of its own because as the game was made for Android it had to be optimized. To make these destructible buildings I used Blender to fracture the models and then using both object pooling and caching to insure optimal runtime. The LOD was also made with object pooling and caching. Put simply, the LOD seperated the map in regions and the render distance is how many of those regions should load in. The buildings would be linked to the best region chosen by the script. To Build the map, I simply gathered a few low polygone models with a few edits on Blender and placed them in a chaotic manner to make are interesting map.
